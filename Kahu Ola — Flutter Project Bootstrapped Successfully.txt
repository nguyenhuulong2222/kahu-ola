### 🎨 Design Decisions
- **Palette**: Deep Ocean Blue (#005B8E) · Lava Orange (#E8601C) · Pali Green (#2E7D32)  
- **Typography**: Nunito headings + Noto Sans body — Kupuna (elderly-friendly) sizing  
- **Splash**: Scale + fade animation → auto-routes to Dashboard after 3 s  
- **Graceful Degradation**: `StaleDataFailure` type shows cached data when any of the 12 government APIs are down  
- **Edge Privacy**: Location never leaves the device (architecture documented in ocean/profile modules)
